
# Category Enum

Indicates the category/type of information. This field is required.

>

> 
> 03 - Fraud alert
> 
> 04 - Operational alert
> 
> 05 - Transactional data
> 
> 06 - Other
> 
> 80 through 99 - PS-specific value (dependent on the payment scheme type)
