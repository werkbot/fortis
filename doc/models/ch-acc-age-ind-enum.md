
# Ch Acc Age Ind Enum

Length of time that the cardholder has had the account with the 3DS Requestor.

> 01 - No account
> 
> 02 - Created during this transaction
> 
> 03 - Less than 30 days
> 
> 04 - Between 30 and 60 days
> 
> 05 - More than 60 days
